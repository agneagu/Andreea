THE SYNOPSIS OF THE MOVIE

Miranda Priestly (Meryl Streep), the most ruthless, critic and powerful executive chief of the famous fashion magazine “Runway”, needs a junior personal assistant. Andrea “Andy” Sachs (Anne Hathaway), an aspiring journalist who got no sense of fashion, obtains the job which “a million girls would kill for” as a second personal assistant of the sophisticate Miranda Priestly. Andrea tries to fit in the fashion world and into Miranda’s expectations so that she asks Nigel (Stanley Tucci), the art director of Runway magazine, to help her understand and grow in the fashion world. Working hard, Andrea gains the respect of her cruel boss, Miranda, but she also loses her private life with her boyfriend, friends and family.
